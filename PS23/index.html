PICT Student Clubs & Extracurricular Activities

Coding Club

The Coding Club is a community of passionate programmers aiming to enhance their coding skills and work on real-world projects.

Achievements: Hackathon winners, Coding Bootcamp completion, Open-source contributions.

Robotics Club

A group dedicated to exploring robotics, designing robots, and participating in national and international robotics competitions.

Achievements: First place in RoboCup, Best Design Award at RoboFest.

Dance Club

Our Dance Club brings together students passionate about dance, learning various dance forms, and performing at college events.

Achievements: Winner of PICT Dance Fest, Annual cultural event performances.
